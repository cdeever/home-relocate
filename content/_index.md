# Home Relocate

A decision-making tool to help find the **best match house** for Sweetie and Chris.

## How It Works

1. **Sweetie's Wishes** - What matters most to Sweetie
2. **Chris's Wishes** - What matters most to Chris
3. **Houses** - Compare properties against our combined criteria

Browse the sections in the left sidebar to explore our preferences and house matches.
