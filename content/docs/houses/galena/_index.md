# Galena

Small village in Delaware County, north of Westerville. Mix of newer subdivisions and rural properties.

## Area Notes
- Delaware County schools (Big Walnut or Olentangy depending on location)
- ~10-15 min to Lewis Center
- More rural/semi-rural feel
- Newer construction (2000s+)
- Lower taxes than Franklin County
