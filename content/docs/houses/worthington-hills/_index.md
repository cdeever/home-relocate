# Worthington Hills

Established golf course community in northwest Columbus (43235). Features Worthington Hills Country Club.

## Area Notes
- Golf course community with mature trees
- Mix of ranches and two-stories from 1960s-80s
- Close to Polaris shopping/dining
- ~10-15 min to Lewis Center
